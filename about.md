# About Me

Hi, I'm Jahin. I'm an amateur graphics designer, programmer, and general nerd about sysadmin phenomena based in Sydney, Australia.

I'm currently studying for a Bachelors of Information Technology degree at Macquarie University. I have had experience in the field of programming and IT, working in laptop refurbishing and repair as part of my Year 10 work experience, and also participating in many hackathons.

I mainly prefer to work with desktop application development. I am most proficient with Python, although I also have experience with Java, and some familiarity with the <abbr title="C, C++, C#">`C*`</abbr> set of languages.

I also have a keen eye towards UI/UX design, ever since I picked up Microsoft PowerPoint all the way back in 2010, when I was still in primary school. I still consider Microsoft PowerPoint to be the best graphics design tool out there for beginners, although I now use [Lunacy](https://icons8.com/lunacy) for most professional work.
